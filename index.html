Dal Makhani

Dal Makhani is a popular Punjabi lentil dish originating from the Indian subcontinent. It's a staple in North Indian cuisine, particularly in Punjab and Delhi.

Taste and Texture
Rich, creamy, slightly spicy, and smooth.

Serving Suggestions
Serve with rice, roti, naan, or paratha.
Accompany with raita (yogurt side dish) or salad.

Vegetable Dhokla

Vegetable Dhokla is a popular Gujarati snack made from fermented gram flour, semolina, and yogurt batter, mixed with grated vegetables and spices. Steamed to perfection, it's soft, spongy, and savory, tempered with mustard seeds and curry leaves. Rich in protein, fiber, and vitamins, this gluten-free snack is perfect for breakfast, snacks, or appetizers, and is a staple at GujaratiÂ gatherings.
